Simple Home Hacks

    Delicious Mocha (pronounced "moca")

    Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon finely ground Marere coffee
  • 500 ml whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons drinking chocolate powder
  • Sugar (optional)

    • Method

  • Bring the milk to a gentle boil.

  • Add the finely ground coffee and drinking chocolate powder.

  • Stir well and sweeten if desired..

  • Pour into a glass cup and enjoy your homemade mocha..

    Thick Banana Smoothie

    Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 300 ml milk
  • 2 ice cubes
  • ½ teaspoon chia seeds (optional)
  • 2 drops vanilla essence (optional)

    • Method

  • Peel and slice the bananas.

  • Place them in a ziplock bag or sealed container and freeze overnight.

  • In a blender, add milk and ice cubes.

  • Add the frozen bananas, chia seeds, and vanilla.

  • Blend for 90 seconds until smooth and creamy.

  • Pour into your favorite glass and enjoy the delicious, thick texture.
